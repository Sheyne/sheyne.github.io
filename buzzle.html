Buzzle

Buzzle is a team drinking game where participants collaborate to each shotgun a beer and solve a 36 piece puzzle.

Important rules:

  • Any regular 12oz carbonated beverage can be shotgunned
  • Puzzle must be a 36 piece Crocodile Creek puzzle
  • Leaderboard can contain only one copy of each pair of names. You can improve your time with a given partner, but it must replace your old time
  • Stopwatch starts before the shotgun begins and ends when the puzzle is completed. Times are truncated to minutes and seconds, fractional seconds are dropped.
  • Leaderboards are always tied to a specific puzzle. When a new puzzle is acquired, you must start a new leaderboard
  • Buzzle is a partner sport, you can only solve the puzzle in the context of a buzzle attempt and can only make a buzzle attempt with a partner
  • When storing the puzzle in the box, all the pieces must be face down so the box can be flipped over and all the pieces are face up
